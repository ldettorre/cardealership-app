Meet The Team

Derek and Susanne O'Neil

Directors of Moderna Motors

Founded in 2011 by Derek and Susanne O'Neil, Moderna Motors has built a reputation for premium quality, unbeatable value, award winning service and market leading Aftercare ™. Having amassed over 3 decades both come from families deeply rooted in the motor industry, they believed that premium service did not always have to cost a premium itself.

Michael Murray

Head of Customer Service

Upon your arrival, you will be met by Michael or one of our Customer Service Executives. Whether you are just looking to browse, find a good fit or jump into a conversation with a particular Sales Executive, Michael and his team will be happy to help in any way they can.

Collette Ryan

Head of Sales

Collette joined Moderna Motors in 2016 as an already well established sales executive within the industry. Her knowledge coupled with the ability to truely understand what a client needs and be able to deliver is a prime example of the service Moderna Motors provides on a daily basis.

Jordan Fox

Head of Aftercare

With over a decade of experience in Aftercare coming from main dealerships, we are proud to have Jordan lead the way in ensuring customer satisfaction post-sale. At Moderna Motors, we believe that Aftercare should never be an Afterthought and encourage all clients to reach out to Jordan with any queries regarding our inventory.

Simon Barry
